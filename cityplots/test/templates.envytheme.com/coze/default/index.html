What are CMDA-approved plots?

CMDA (Chennai Metropolitan Development Authority) approved plots are residential or commercial land parcels that have received official approval from the CMDA in Chennai, India, indicating that they meet the necessary legal and regulatory requirements for construction and development.

A residential plot is a designated area of land intended for the construction of homes or apartments. It is specifically allocated for residential purposes.
